How EV is Calculated

Step 1 — Consensus "true probability": We fetch player home run lines and average implied probability across all tracked books and scraped fallbacks to build a market consensus probability.

Step 2 — Book implied probability: We convert each book's raw American odds into implied probability.

Step 3 — EV% formula: EV% = (TrueProb × Payout) − (1 − TrueProb) where Payout is the decimal payout from that specific book's line (e.g. +200 → 2.0x).

Step 4 — Alerts: If a specific book line EV% meets your threshold, a Pushover notification is sent with player, book, odds, and EV%.
