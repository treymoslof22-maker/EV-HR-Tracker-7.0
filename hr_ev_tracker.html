How EV is Calculated

Step 1 — Consensus "true probability": We fetch HR To Score (anytime) props from all available sportsbooks via The Odds API. We remove vig from each book's line using the standard no-vig formula, then average those probabilities across all non-BetMGM books to get a market consensus true probability.

Step 2 — BetMGM implied probability: We take BetMGM Colorado's raw American odds and convert them to implied probability (without removing vig).

Step 3 — EV% formula: EV% = (TrueProb × Payout) − (1 − TrueProb) where Payout is the decimal payout from BetMGM's line (e.g. +200 → 2.0x).

Step 4 — Alert: If EV% ≥ your threshold, a Pushover notification is sent to your phone with the player, odds, and EV%.
